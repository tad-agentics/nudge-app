07 — Usage Rules

Do

  • Use the warm stripe as a top-bar accent (4px)
  • Maintain generous whitespace (24px min)
  • Use 16px border-radius on all cards
  • Use Ink Black for primary actions
  • Let the cream background breathe
  • Use the standalone N for small contexts

Don't

  • Use left-border accents on cards (AI design tell)
  • Use the stripe as a full background
  • Add gradients beyond the stripe
  • Use blue anywhere in the product
  • Add illustrations, mascots, or emoji
  • Use pure white (#FFF) as background
  • Animate the stripe or logo mark
